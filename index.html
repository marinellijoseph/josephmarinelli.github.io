Joseph Marinelli

Joseph Marinelli is a member of the Operations Finance team at Clark Construction Group and ensures that multiple construction projects in the DMV are financed promptly and correctly. Joseph is familiar with many parts of the construction industry, and excels in maintaining order and sanity throughout all stages of a construction project. His main responsibilities are the Financial Management of these projects to measure profitability, schedule, financial risk and potential exposures. Joseph is currently enrolled in GW’s Data Analytics coding bootcamp. Upon graduation he is looking for a company where he can begin a new career in Data Analytics or Web Development. His ideal company embraces his desire to learn as much as he can and is willing to foster his professional and personal growth. In his freetime Joseph loves to play and watch sports, and enjoys video games on all platforms. Joseph holds a Bachelor’s degree in Finance from James Madison University.
